## Why NonCommercial?

The audio stimuli and the IRT item parameters in `calibration.json` were
calibrated against a specific population of English-as-a-Foreign-Language
learners. Reusing the calibrated parameters for a commercial assessment of a
different population without recalibration would compromise measurement
validity. Researchers who need to apply LJT-CAT in a new commercial context
should re-calibrate on their own data and seek an explicit commercial
licensing arrangement; please contact the maintainers via the project's
GitHub issues page.
